Consultancy Scope

Mining and Mineral Processing Engineering Services

Technical support for mine planning, crushing systems, maintenance, production improvement, engineering design, and practical training packages.

Crushing Plant Design

JEA Consultancy Firm develops practical crushing circuit concepts, equipment selection guidance, layout recommendations, and production-focused improvements for hard rock and aggregate operations.

Pit Design and Optimization

Services include open pit geometry, haul road planning, bench layout, operating sequence review, and pit optimization strategies for safer, more efficient mining.

Crusher Maintenance

Maintenance support covers jaw crushers, cone crushers, vibrating screens, conveyor systems, failure analysis, inspection planning, and downtime reduction.

Technical Drawing and Design

Deliverables include plant layouts, mechanical drawings, CAD concepts, equipment arrangement drawings, and engineering diagrams prepared for clear technical communication.

Professional Training Packages

Training covers technical drawing and mining software, mine pit design and planning, and drilling and blasting engineering, with practical calculations, templates, field documents, and project exercises.

Work Method

From Site Problem to Engineering Action

The consultancy process begins with understanding site constraints, production targets, operating bottlenecks, equipment condition, and safety requirements. Recommendations are then translated into practical designs, maintenance actions, or production improvement plans.
